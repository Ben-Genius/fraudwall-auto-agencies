## üîç RESEARCH STRATEGY

1.  **Broad Patterns**: Start with "Essential" sources (Mobbin, Awwwards, Apple HIG).
2.  **Project Specific**: Use recommended sources for the specific asset (e.g., SaaSpo for dashboards).
3.  **Specialized Deep-Dive**: Explore focus areas like motion (UI Movement) or onboarding (UserOnboard).
4.  **Cross-Reference**: Validate patterns across at least 3 sources for consistency and trend-alignment.
